Overview Therapy Services

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): Focuses on identifying and changing negative thought patterns and behaviors.
  • Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT): Helps individuals manage emotions and improve relationships through mindfulness and coping strategies
  • Psychodynamic Therapy: Explores unconscious thoughts and childhood experiences to understand current behavior and emotions.
  • Humanistic Therapy: Emphasizes personal growth and self-actualization, often through techniques like client-centered therapy.

  • Exposure Therapy: Gradually exposes individuals to feared situations or objects to reduce anxiety.
  • Operant Conditioning: Uses reinforcement and punishment to modify behavior

Focuses on changing distorted thinking patterns and beliefs that contribute to emotional distress.

  • Systemic Therapy: Addresses family dynamics and communication issues.
  • Structural Family Therapy: Examines and alters family structures and hierarchies.

  • Art Therapy: Uses creative expression to explore emotions and issues.
  • Play Therapy: Utilizes play to help children express and process feelings.
  • Music Therapy: Involves using music to address emotional, cognitive, and social needs.
  • Animal-Assisted Therapy: Incorporates animals to improve emotional and physical well-being.


Frequently Asked Questions

Are physiotherapy treatments painful?

Physiotherapy treatments are not excessively painful. Some techniques might cause mild discomfort.

What should I wear to my physiotherapy appointments?

Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated. Athletic wear is usually a good choice.

What are the costs of physiotherapy services?

Costs can vary based on the type of service, duration, and location. It’s best to check with the clinic for specific pricing and payment options.

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, you’ll have an initial assessment where the physiotherapist will review your medical history, discuss your concerns, and perform an evaluation to develop a personalized treatment plan.

How can physiotherapy benefit me?

Physiotherapy can help alleviate pain, improve mobility, enhance physical function, and prevent future injuries through tailored exercise programs and hands-on treatments..
